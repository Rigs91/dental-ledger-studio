# 60-Second Demo Script

1. Start on the sign-in screen and click `Sign in`.

2. On the dashboard:
   "This is Dental Ledger Studio, a ledger-first dental billing and claims-review product. In one screen I can see balances at risk, open flags, insurance ambiguity, and which patients need attention next."

3. In `Balances to watch`, open `Maria Chen 41`.

4. On the patient profile:
   "This page combines balance snapshot, patient activity, procedures, insurance history, and open review flags. The key product idea is that the balance is explained by ledger events rather than a stored balance field."

5. Open the denied claim from the patient workflow.

6. On the billing timeline:
   "The claim keeps the insurance used, the current balance, the claim decision path, and the submission history together. If the team resubmits the claim, the new context is appended instead of overwriting the original record."

7. Open `Analytics` from the top navigation.

8. On analytics:
   "Analytics here are operational, not vanity metrics. The product surfaces what needs review next, where revenue is stuck, and which root causes are driving follow-up work."
